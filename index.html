Featured Projects

NetherB0T (Owner)

NetherB0T is a Minecraft bot hosted using Mineflayer, a JavaScript library that interacts with Minecraft. It features over 30 commands and has been used by more than 75,000 unique players across 19 servers throughout its lifetime. Over 1,250 facts have been submitted through its addfact command.

WebClaimMap (Owner)

WebClaimMap was developed to scrape Dynmap and Bluemap web maps in Minecraft to track details such as nearby claimable land, land ownership, balances, member counts, and more. It featured a local web GUI for changing config and managing data, along with Discord webhook integrations.

8b8t.me (Developer)

8b8t is the largest non-vanilla Minecraft anarchy server with no rules. Its 5,000 GB map has been running for over five years, with more than one million unique player joins and an average of 80 concurrent players.

I developed custom anti-lag plugins to prevent crashes and notify server owners. I also contributed to smaller servers such as 6b6t.ca and 2l2c.org.
